Screen 8 — Field & Behaviour Notes
▸ Affected Bookings: Shows ONLY bookings with a payout date within the next 14 days — i.e., at risk of being affected by the current rate movement.
▸ Rate Difference: Displayed as %. Highlighted red when unfavourable to Hairline (i.e., provider currency has strengthened against patient currency, increasing payout cost).
▸ Estimated Exposure: Auto-calculated potential gain/loss to Hairline from the rate movement. Negative = loss (shown in red); positive = gain (shown in green).
▸ Booking Reference: Clickable link — navigates to the booking record in A-01.
▸ Decision (radio): Mandatory. Selecting "Adjust commission configuration" does not initiate the edit — it routes admin to the appropriate surface (FR-015 / A-02 or FR-029 / A-09).
▸ Notes (textarea): Mandatory whenever any decision is selected. Max 500 characters.
▸ Confirm Decision: Saves decision + notes to Transaction Audit Log (Entity 5) with action_type = "Currency Alert Decision". Dismisses modal and removes alert from notification dropdown.
▸ Close button: Dismisses without saving. Admin can return via notification history. Alert remains active in the notification dropdown.
▸ Re-alert: If rate continues to diverge and a new alert fires (FR-029 / A-09), the alert re-surfaces even after a prior decision was confirmed.
